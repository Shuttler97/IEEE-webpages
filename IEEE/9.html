If not now, when? In these changing times, the best investment you can make is in yourself – that’s why is so important.
You have heard the saying, “Necessity is the mother of invention”. We see the signs of necessity now more than ever, in the need for cleaner, better, more efficient and less expensive technology and innovation.
That is where IEEE can bring you together with the best and brightest in the world of engineering and technology.
As a Undergraduate Student in India the following benefits will provide you with the most rewarding IEEE experience:

  • Keeping technically current

  • Career resources and Recognition

  • Professional Networking

  • Continuing Education

  • Discounts on IEEE conference and publications
