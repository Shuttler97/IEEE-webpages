About IEEE Student Branch @ AKGEC

IEEE Student Branch AKGEC, Ghaziabad (Branch Code: 10651) was formed on July 18, 2012. The petition for the formation of the student branch was submitted by the students of AKGEC, Ghaziabad early in the year 2012. IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC endeavors to promote awareness among students on the opportunities that exists in the field of computer science and engineering and to organize IEEE activities on a regular basis along with healthy participation from members of various student branches. We aim to provide interactive platform for students to develop their professional and technical abilities.
