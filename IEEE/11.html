    Previous

  • The IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC had organized a seminar on “Computer Awareness and its Applications for Education in Rural Areas” to celebrate the 45th Engineers Day on 15th September 2012, to commemorate the birthday of Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvarayya, an eminent Indian engineer. This 45th Engineers Day was dedicated to “Appropriate Climate-Responsive Technologies for Inclusive Growth and Sustainable Development”, considering global awareness on climate change and realizing Engineers' role in economic development and environmental responsibility. Engineers Day should be a day in which all jested minds in Engineering sector should be treated with zealous by the authorities; otherwise this day will be disappear from history as time passes.
