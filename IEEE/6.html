From Branch Counselor Desk…

As the branch co-counselor of this student branch, I hope that this IEEE Student Branch is showcasing the inherent talent of all the students, not only of their research and technical capabilities but innovative aspects as well.
IEEE student branch was established in order to create awareness amongst faculties, students and all other peoples about the activities of IEEE and Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College. Thus, it is really my pleasure and privilege to get associated with the IEEE Student Branch of Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College, Ghaziabad under UP Section.
I cheer the members for bringing out such an association which provide a platform for students to display their talent. I wish the Student Branch, faculties and the student members all success in their effort.

Dr. Sunita Yadav

Branch Co-Counselor, IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC Ghaziabad
Prof., Dept. of CSE, AKGEC, Ghaziabad
