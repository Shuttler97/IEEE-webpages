From Branch Counselor Desk…

I am glad to see that IEEE Student Branch of AKGEC is serving the society as a whole by imparting knowledge far and wide. I hope the branch will continue the same trend in the near future. In a technical institute like ours it is good to see students getting involved in core technical learning and projects.
IEEE has become a real communicator and facilitator amongst all the scientific and technical communities which are striving for the betterment of this world. As an information technology research institute, AKGEC would also like to play an important role of counterpart in this process. AKGEC has always been a keen activity organizer in the areas related to technology and professional growth of an individual.
To make IEEE student branch at AKGEC a success, we shall really need a kind collaborative support from all the members and technical knowledge hunters. This support is incorporated by active and meaningful participation in the branch activities. With the same desire, I invite you all to extend your support and join us in the activities related to IEEE at our student branch.
I wish the IEEE Student Branch for all their future endeavors.

Prof. Mamta Bhusry

Branch Counselor, IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC, Ghaziabad,
HoD, Dept. of Computer Science & Engg., AKGEC, Ghaziabad
