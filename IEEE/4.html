From EE Head Desk...

It is a matter of immense pleasure that the IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC has organized different events in the past years including IEEE International Conference, seminars, industrial visits, workshops etc. I wish that IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC will continue with the traditions of IEEE, to disseminate information and knowledge from this forum among its student members.
I hope that students from AKGEC, Ghaziabad will continue to exhibit their interest by participating in all events organized by the executive body of IEEE Student Branch, AKGEC.

Dr. R.K. Agarwal

EE Department Head, IEEE Student Branch,
AKGEC Director , AKGEC, Ghaziabad
